The Best Movies According to Ilyas

My top 3 movies of all-time.

3 Idiots

I love the movie for its college backdrop and how the movie rovolves around the main character who in disguise turns out to be the topper of the college

Ye Jawaani hai Deewani

The movie is a love story, wherein the lead actor is behind his goals and the actress is a girl next door who falls for him

Taare Zameen par

A child who has issues reading and writing find a teacher who recognizes his true talent and makes him confident to present in to the world for others to know his true potential
